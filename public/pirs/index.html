Priority Intelligence Requirements

PIR 1 - Regional Power Competition#

What indicators reveal shifts in strategic alignment, influence or posture among Pacific nations and how do these shifts affect the regional security environment?

PIR 2 - China’s Military, Economic and Influence Activities in the Pacific#

How is China expanding its diplomatic, economic and security footprint across the Pacific. What are the implications for regional stability and strategic interests?

PIR 3 - Domestic Stability in Pacific Nations#

What political, economic or governance indicators signal rising instability or leadership/civilian vulnerability risks in Pacific nations.

PIR 4 - Cyber Threat Evolution Affecting The Pacific Region#

How are state and non-state cyber actors targeting Pacific critical infrastructure, government and business. What emerging vulnerabilities warrant early warning?

PIR 5 - Transnational Organised Crime & Illicit Finance Across the Pacific#

How are transnational crime groups adapting their methods in narcotics trafficking, fraud, corruption and money laundering across the region? What vulnerabilities are emerging?

PIR 6 - Critical Infrastructure Stress & Supply Chain Vulnerabilities#

What emerging threats or disruptions are affecting energy, water transport, communications and logistic systems across the Pacific region?

PIR 7 - Climate Change, Natural Hazards & Regional Resilience#

How are climate-driven events impacting domestic stability, humanitarian needs and regional security?

PIR 8 - Defense Capability Developments & Regional Posture Shifts#

How are Pacific nations enhancing or repositioning military capabilities, readiness or posture and how do these developments reshape the strategic balance?

PIR 9 - Emerging Technologies & Security Implications#

What developments in AI, cyber tools, surveillance tech autonomous systems and dual-use technologies present new security risks or opportunities across the Pacific?
